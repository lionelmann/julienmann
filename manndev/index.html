How It Works 03 / 04
  • 01 Initial Enquiry +
    Fill in the contact form or email me directly. Tell me what you need — even a rough idea is enough to start. I'll get back to you within 24 hours with initial thoughts and availability.
  • 02 Discovery Call +
    A 30–60 minute call to understand your goals, audience, and what you need the site to do. We'll talk through examples, timelines, and budget. No obligation — if it's not the right fit, I'll say so.
  • 03 Proposal & Scope +
    A written proposal covering exactly what's included, the timeline, and the fixed price. No hourly rates, no scope creep surprises. A deposit confirms the booking and locks in your start date.
  • 04 Design & Build +
    I design and build in parallel, sharing progress at key checkpoints. You'll see a live preview before anything goes public. Feedback rounds are built into the schedule so there's time to refine.
  • 05 Launch & Handover +
    Once you're happy, I deploy to your domain. You get full access to everything — code, hosting, accounts. I'll walk you through how to manage it and stay available for 30 days post-launch at no extra charge.
